KIM WOO BIN

Kim Woo-bin (born Kim Hyun-joong on July 16, 1989) is a South Korean actor and model. He began his career as a runway model and made his acting debut in the television drama White Christmas. He subsequently gained attention in A Gentleman's Dignity (2012), and made his breakthrough with The Heirs (2013).In 2016, he took on his first leading role on television in Uncontrollably Fond. He was just diagnosed with Nasopharynx cancer. Cancer survivor Kim Woo-bin continues series comeback with Netflix's 'Black Knight'
