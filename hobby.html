Cafe Hunting

Cafe hopping simply means visiting more than one cafe in a day or during a single outing, trying out basically every cafe in town! For every new cafe, a new type of coffee shop customer emerges. Cafe hoppers are consumers who enjoy going to a cafe or enjoy cafe hopping once in a while.
